NIGHTS LIST

Join our expert panels over 5 nights as they focus on 5 different facets of Human Rights in Australia:

  1. The rights of Indigenous Australians (Monday 5 December at Australia Hall);
  2. Privacy rights (Tuesday 6 December at Colin Biggers & Paisley);
  3. Women's health rights (Wednesday 7 December at the University of New South Wales);
  4. Disability rights (Thursday 8 December at University of Technology Sydney); and
  5. Refugee rights (Friday 9 December at the University of Sydney).
All events are free to the public but please register through Eventbrite to secure your ticket, as seats for each event are limited. There will also be an opportunity to speak with the panelists and other organisations who work in these areas if you are interested in finding out how you can get involved in promoting these rights.
